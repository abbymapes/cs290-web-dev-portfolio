Clever Hangman


Welcome to Clever Hangman. It's like Hangman, but when you guess a letter, I will change the secret word I'm thinking of to make the game harder for you. Want to play? Write your name below, and select a difficulty and a game mode. Easy will give you 20 guesses to guess my word and hard will give you 15 guesses. The debugging mode allows you to see why my algorithm is "clever," by providing information about the choices I make for each guess.
